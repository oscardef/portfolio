## Context

Bloomberg builds the infrastructure that powers global financial markets. Their systems handle massive volumes of real-time data and support workflows that financial institutions around the world depend on every day.

I'll be joining the London engineering office for a six-month placement starting March 2026, on the Core Communications Distribution team.

## What I'll Be Working On

The Core Communications Distribution team builds infrastructure for real-time event distribution — the systems that Bloomberg's IB (Instant Bloomberg) and MSG platforms rely on to move data between services quickly and reliably.

Day-to-day, this means contributing to the design and development of scalable, low-latency systems in a high-performance production environment. Think real-time messaging infrastructure, event routing, and the kind of backend work where milliseconds matter.
